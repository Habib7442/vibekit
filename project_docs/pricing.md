## The Problem: One-Time Use Case

### User Journey:
1. Month 1: Sign up, create media kit → **Pays $15**
2. Month 2: Already has media kit → **Cancels subscription**
3. Months 3-12: Uses the same media kit for free

**Result:** You get $15 instead of $180/year. Not sustainable.

---

## Solutions: Make Monthly Subscription Valuable

### Strategy 1: **Consumable Credits Model** (Best for this use case)

Instead of unlimited monthly access, give **credits** that refresh:

```
FREE TIER:
- 3 AI generations ever (one-time)
- 1 hosted media kit
- No updates after creation

PRO TIER - $19/month:
- 20 AI generations per month (refreshes monthly)
- Unlimited hosted media kits
- Unlimited updates/refinements
- Analytics dashboard
- Custom domain

Why this works:
- Creators need to UPDATE their stats every month
- They tweak designs as brands give feedback
- They create different kits for different pitches
```

### Strategy 2: **Must-Have Monthly Features**

Build features that REQUIRE monthly subscription:

```
Features that create monthly dependency:

1. AUTO-UPDATING STATS
   - Sync with Instagram monthly
   - Media kit stats always current
   - Without subscription: Stats frozen at creation date
   
2. ANALYTICS TRACKING
   - "Your media kit was viewed 47 times this month"
   - "3 brands downloaded your PDF"
   - "Most views from: United States"
   - Without subscription: No analytics
   
3. CUSTOM DOMAIN
   - yourname.com points to media kit
   - Without subscription: Reverts to yourname.imagestudiolab.com
   
4. AI PITCH GENERATOR
   - Generate custom pitches for brands monthly
   - "Brainstorm brand partnership ideas"
   - Without subscription: Can't generate new pitches
   
5. MULTIPLE MEDIA KITS
   - Different kits for different brand types
   - "Fashion brands kit" vs "Beauty brands kit"
   - Without subscription: Only 1 kit active
```

### Strategy 3: **Freemium with Paid Add-Ons**

```
FREE FOREVER:
- Create 1 basic media kit
- Limited templates
- imagestudiolab.com subdomain
- No analytics

ONE-TIME PURCHASES:
- Premium template pack: $29 one-time
- Custom domain setup: $49 one-time
- Professional design consultation: $99 one-time

MONTHLY PRO ($19/mo):
- Unlimited AI generations
- Priority support
- Advanced analytics
- Auto-updating stats
- Remove branding
```

### Strategy 4: **Usage-Based Pricing** (Most Fair)

```
Pay only for what you use:

- Media kit creation: $10 each
- AI refinements: $2 per iteration
- Hosting: $5/month per kit
- Custom domain: $3/month
- Analytics: $5/month
- PDF exports: $3 each

Example monthly cost:
- Creator uses 1 hosted kit: $5/mo
- Tweaks it 2 times: $4
- Wants analytics: $5/mo
Total: $14/mo (similar to subscription but feels fairer)
```

## Why Creators Will Keep Paying Monthly:

### Reason 1: **Stats Get Stale**
- Month 1: 45K followers, 4.8% engagement
- Month 3: 52K followers, 5.2% engagement
- **Problem:** Media kit shows old numbers, looks unprofessional
- **Solution:** Pro subscription auto-updates monthly

### Reason 2: **Multiple Use Cases**
Creators need different kits for:
- Fashion brands (show fashion content)
- Beauty brands (show beauty content)
- Agencies (comprehensive portfolio)
- Quick one-pager (for DMs)

### Reason 3: **Analytics Are Addictive**
Once creators see:
- "Your media kit was viewed 47 times last month"
- "3 brands from your target list viewed it"
- "Most popular section: Portfolio"

They won't want to lose this data.

### Reason 4: **Professional Image**
- Free tier: `maya-sharma-2847.imagestudiolab.com`

Serious creators pay for the professional URL.
